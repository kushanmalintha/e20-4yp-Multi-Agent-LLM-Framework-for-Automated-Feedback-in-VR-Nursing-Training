CLEANING AND DRESSING PREPARATION GUIDELINES
Clean Surgical Wound Care

PURPOSE
To prepare all equipment, solutions, and materials safely and correctly before wound care.
This is a PREPARATION step only - no actual wound cleaning or dressing application occurs.

CLINICAL CONTEXT
- Wound type: Clean surgical wound
- Location: Left forearm
- Prescribed cleaning solution: Surgical spirit
- Prescribed dressing type: Dry dressing

CRITICAL PRINCIPLES
1. Infection control through repeated hand hygiene
2. Safe verification with staff nurse (mandatory)
3. Correct sequencing of preparation actions
4. No assumptions - every action must be explicit

REQUIRED ACTIONS IN SEQUENCE

Action: action_initial_hand_hygiene
Description: Initial Hand Hygiene
- Wash hands according to infection control guidelines
- Purpose: Prevents contamination before touching equipment
- CRITICAL: Must be done BEFORE any equipment contact
- Prerequisites: None (first action)

Action: action_clean_trolley
Description: Clean the Dressing Trolley
- Clean and disinfect the trolley surface
- Ensure surface is visibly clean and dry
- Purpose: Removes environmental contaminants
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene

Action: action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning
Description: Hand Hygiene After Trolley Cleaning
- Wash hands again after trolley cleaning
- Purpose: Prevents re-contamination after contact with cleaning surfaces
- CRITICAL: Hand hygiene is required AFTER cleaning tasks
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley

Action: action_select_solution
Description: Select Prescribed Cleaning Solution
- Take the prescribed cleaning solution (surgical spirit)
- Purpose: Ensures correct solution per medical orders
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning

Action: action_verify_solution
Description: Verify Cleaning Solution with Staff Nurse (MANDATORY)
- Check solution name
- Check expiration date
- Check bottle integrity
- Verbally confirm with staff nurse
- Purpose: Prevents medication errors
- CRITICAL: Cannot proceed without staff nurse verification
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution

Action: action_select_dressing
Description: Select Dressing Materials
- Take required dressing materials
- Ensure dressing type matches prescription (dry dressing)
- Purpose: Ensures appropriate materials prepared
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution, action_verify_solution

Action: action_verify_dressing
Description: Verify Sterile Dressing Packet with Staff Nurse (MANDATORY)
- Check packet integrity (no tears, no moisture)
- Check expiration date
- Verbally confirm sterility with staff nurse
- Purpose: Maintains aseptic technique
- CRITICAL: Cannot proceed without staff nurse verification
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution, action_verify_solution, action_select_dressing

Action: action_arrange_materials
Description: Arrange Solutions and Materials on Trolley
- Place verified items neatly on trolley
- Organize for efficient workflow
- Purpose: Ensures organized, efficient setup
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution, action_verify_solution, action_select_dressing, action_verify_dressing

Action: action_bring_trolley
Description: Bring Prepared Trolley to Patient Area
- Transport prepared trolley to bedside/treatment area
- Purpose: Marks completion of preparation
- CRITICAL: Final action confirming readiness
- Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution, action_verify_solution, action_select_dressing, action_verify_dressing, action_arrange_materials

PREREQUISITE MAPPING FOR REAL-TIME FEEDBACK

When evaluating if a student can perform an action, check ALL prerequisites:

action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning
  Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley

action_select_dressing

action_verify_dressing
  Prerequisites: action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution, action_verify_solution, action_select_dressing

  This is the final action and requires complete preparation

REAL-TIME FEEDBACK EXAMPLES

Example 1: Student does action_initial_hand_hygiene, then jumps to action_verify_solution
Completed: [action_initial_hand_hygiene]
Current: action_verify_solution
Missing: action_clean_trolley, action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution
Feedback: "Before verifying the cleaning solution, you must: clean the trolley, perform hand hygiene after cleaning, and select the cleaning solution."

Example 2: Student does action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley, then jumps to action_verify_dressing
Completed: [action_initial_hand_hygiene, action_clean_trolley]
Current: action_verify_dressing
Missing: action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning, action_select_solution, action_verify_solution, action_select_dressing
Feedback: "Before verifying the dressing packet, you must: perform hand hygiene after trolley cleaning, select cleaning solution, verify solution with nurse, and select dressing materials."

Example 3: Student does actions in correct sequence - action_initial_hand_hygiene, then action_clean_trolley
Completed: [action_initial_hand_hygiene]
Current: action_clean_trolley
Missing: None
Feedback: "Good! Cleaning the trolley is the correct next step after hand hygiene."

ACTIONS NOT INCLUDED IN THIS STEP
- Actual wound cleaning
- Actual dressing application
- Touching the wound
- Removing existing dressings
- Applying solutions to wound

This step is PREPARATORY ONLY.

EVALUATION CRITERIA

MANDATORY ACTIONS (Cannot be skipped):
1. action_initial_hand_hygiene - Hand hygiene before equipment contact
2. action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning - Hand hygiene after trolley cleaning
3. action_verify_solution - Cleaning solution verification with staff nurse
4. action_verify_dressing - Dressing packet verification with staff nurse

CRITICAL SEQUENCE VIOLATIONS:
- Touching equipment before action_initial_hand_hygiene
- Skipping action_hand_hygiene_after_cleaning
- Not performing action_verify_solution or action_verify_dressing
- Performing action_bring_trolley before completing preparation
- Performing any action without completing its prerequisites

PATIENT SAFETY PRIORITIES:
1. Infection control (hand hygiene compliance)
2. Medication safety (solution verification)
3. Aseptic technique (sterile material verification)
4. Correct sequencing (prevents cross-contamination)

COMMON ERRORS TO DETECT:
- Missing hand hygiene steps
- Skipping staff nurse verification
- Incorrect solution selection
- Using expired materials
- Wrong dressing type
- Improper sequencing (jumping ahead without prerequisites)
- Incomplete preparation before bringing trolley

FEEDBACK PRINCIPLES:
- Real-time feedback when ANY prerequisite is missing
- List ALL missing prerequisites, not just the immediate previous action
- Non-blocking - student can continue but must understand gaps
- Formative approach focusing on learning
- Emphasizes patient safety implications

EDUCATIONAL FOCUS:
This step teaches that errors during preparation can compromise patient safety
even before wound care begins. Safe preparation is a patient-safety responsibility.
Every prerequisite exists for a reason - skipping any step creates risk.
